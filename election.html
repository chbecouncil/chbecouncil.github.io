2nd Year Representative

This position is an ongoing involvement in which the student will represent the entire 2nd year student class in the CHBE Undergraduate Society. As a 2nd Year Rep, you will be the primary point of contact for students in your class and you will deal with any concerns they may have and bring them up to the CHBE council and/or the department. Also, you will be asked to participate in the Undergraduate Handbook. This is a very fun and motivating position to start getting involved in your Undergraduate Society.

Feel free to contact last year’s 2nd Year Representatives if you wish to learn more about this position. They are the current Vice-presidents Gillian Fung-Quon and Logan Trevor Phillips on the Team.

How to run for CHBE 2nd Year Representative?

1- September 9 th – 16 th : Call for nomination
  1.   a. Send your name and contact information to president@chbecouncil.com
  2.  b. Also send your bio if you wish to have it posted on our website (optional)
  3.  c. Note that the deadline to send your nomination is September 15th at 5 pm
2- September 9 th – 22 nd : Campaign
  1.  a. Tell everyone about your great ideas
  2.  b. You can use social media and post your experience and ideas in the CHBE clubroom
  3.  c. Feel free to talk to any of the CHBE Undergraduate Club
3- September 19th – 22nd : Voting
  1.  a. Voting will open on September 19th and it will be done online through the EUS voting system. (a link will be provided and posted on the facebook page and the website)
  2.  b. Voting will end on September 22nd at 5 pm
4- September 23rd : Election result
  1.  a. The winning candidate will be announced at the CHBE lunch on September 23rd .

CHBE Officers - Job Descriptions
Deadline date: September 16th 2016
Applications to be submitted to vicepresident@chbecouncil.com

Publicity officer (2)

This position is a project-based involvement and tasks and responsibilities will vary from time to time. Publicity officers' responsibilities include (but are not limited to):
  • -Prepare all posters for the club
  • -Be responsible for social media announcements
  • -Create, publish and share monthly newsletters
  • -Help make pages to be submitted to the engineering year book
  • -Keep the event calendar up-to-date

Facility officers (2)

This position is a project-based involvement and tasks and responsibilities will vary from time to time. Facilities Officers' responsibilities include (but are not limited to):
  • -Organize locker assignments and payments
  • -Ensure the club has stock of items it sells including clothing items
  • -Maintain all areas controlled by the club, such as the Club room

Safety and Sustainability officer (2)

This position is a project-based involvement and tasks and responsibilities will vary from time to time. Sustainability Officer's responsibilities include (but are not limited to):
  • -Attend monthly department Safety council meeting with the department
  • -Be the 3rd floor emergency marshal
  • -Promote and support CHBE sustainability
  • -Coordinate with other sustainable initiatives on campus
  • -Work with the department, the academic reps and the facility officers to ensure sustainability and awareness within the department

Spirit officers (2)

This position is a project-based involvement and tasks and responsibilities will vary from time to time. Spirit Officer's responsibilities include (but are not limited to):
  • -Motivate Club Members to participate in all sorts of events (CHBE and non-CHBE)
  • -Be in charge of making the ball model for e-ball
  • -Assist the club in recruiting volunteers for CHBE events (BBQs for example) and for E-Week

Equity officer

This position is a project-based involvement and tasks and responsibilities will vary from time to time. Facilities Officers' responsibilities include (but are not limited to):
  • -Assist treasurer with managing funds
  • Assist council with selling merchandise at CHBE events
  • -Organise and run fundraising events for CHBE council

Photographer

As the official CHBE Club photographer, you will be invited to all sorts of events (free of cost) where CHBE students are represented. Take pictures that represent the spirit of CHBE students in social, sporty, academic and more professional settings. You will be in charge of uploading the pictures and any picture may be used for posters or Department publications.
This position requires you to have your own camera.
Official CHBE photographer will be offered a photography course provided by the EUS.

Social Rep officer position

    Coordinate social events with the Social Coordinator
  • - Obtain Liquor licenses and food licenses
  • - Be creative in organizing fun events for everyone
  • - Food Safe and Serving It right are not required but great assets

    • How do you Apply?

    Simple, send your resume at vicepresident@chbecouncil.com and tell us why you would be the best candidate for this position.
    Some of the positions will require 2 people. Therefore, you can apply as a team of 2 or by yourself. If you apply as a single candidate, you will be matched to another person if need be.
    Feel free to contact any member of the CHBE Undergraduate Club if you have any more questions.

    Contacting the Council

    The CHBE Council values your thoughts and opinions on all our activities. Should you have any ideas or questions, please feel free to talk to any council member whenever possible. Our email addresses are also available on the Team page.
