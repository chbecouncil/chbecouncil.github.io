About the Club

The UBC Chemical and Biological Engineering Undergraduate Student Club, made up of elected executives and appointed officers, strives to improve the academic and non-academic experiences of CHBE students by organizing various activities and initiatives. The Club makes sure that your voice is being heard and expectations are being met. Some of our main activities are:

CHBoo Halloween Social 2015: The Bimini Public House

CHBE Industry Night Term 1 2015: Coppertank Broadway

CHBE Field Trip 2015: Shell Caroline Gas Complex, Calgary

  • Social Events

    We know you are busy studying but your CHBE Undergraduate Club knows how to throw a great party. We also do monthly CHB-BQs, game nights and our famous MEXICHEM !!! Check the Events tab to see what is coming up!

  • Industry Night

    The world is rough out there, which is why your CHBE Undergraduate Club organizes industry mixers, professional panels, information sessions, and workshop. Make sure to check the Event tab to see what is next!

  • 3rd Year Field Trip

    CHBE students are very lucky to have the opportunity to go visit some process plants and meet industry professionals. During your 3rd year in CHBE, you will have the opportunity to go on a week-long field trip and visit plenty of industrial facilities. Also, in the winter term, local field trips will be orffered to students. Contact your 3rd year Rep for more info!
